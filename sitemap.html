Interactive Simulations

  • Conduction Simulation -

    A lattice of atoms which vibrate and transfer thermal energy through direct molecular interaction, demonstrating heat conduction principles.

  • Convection Simulation -

    A simple box of fluid which responds to heating and cooling, demonstrating thermal energy transfer through fluid movement.

    A black-body radiator transmitting thermal energy through by electromagnetic radiation

  • Maxwell's Demon Simulation -

    A box of particles with a gate controlled by a demon, exploring entropy and the second law of thermodynamics.
