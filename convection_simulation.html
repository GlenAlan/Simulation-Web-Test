About this Simulation

This simulation visualizes fluid convection. Hotter fluid (red) rises, and cooler fluid (blue) sinks, creating circulation patterns. Click and drag on the canvas to apply heat locally.

Adjust parameters using the controls panel (toggle with the icon). Press 'F' to toggle the FPS display. Use the fullscreen icon () for an immersive view.

The simulation solves a simplified version of the Navier-Stokes equations for fluid dynamics, incorporating buoyancy effects due to temperature differences. It demonstrates concepts like advection (transport of heat by fluid motion) and diffusion (spreading of heat).
