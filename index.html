Interactive Science Playground

Welcome to simulations.science, your interactive physics and science playground. We offer a collection of elegant and easy-to-use simulations designed to make complex scientific concepts tangible and intuitive. Our interactive tools help students, teachers, and science enthusiasts visualize phenomena like heat conduction, convection, and thermodynamics. Whether you're studying physics, teaching science, or simply curious about how the world works, our tools are here to help you explore, experiment, and experience science in a whole new way.

Convection

A simple box of fluid which responds to heating and cooling, demonstrating thermal energy transfer

Conduction

A lattice of atoms which vibrate and transfer thermal energy through direct molecular interaction

Radiation

Explore how thermal energy transfers through electromagnetic waves without a medium, demonstrating photon emission and absorption

Maxwell's Demon

A box of particles with a gate controlled by a demon, exploring entropy and the second law of thermodynamics

Buoyancy
